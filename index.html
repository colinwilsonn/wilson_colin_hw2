Stan Marsh

Stan Marsh is the everyman of the group, known for his common sense and moral compass. He often finds himself questioning the crazy situations his friends get into, making him a voice of reason in South Park.

X

Kyle Broflovski

Kyle Broflovski is the intelligent and principled member of the group. Often clashing with Cartman, Kyle's strong sense of justice and logical thinking drive many of the show's moral dilemmas.

X

Eric Cartman

Eric Cartman is the group's selfish and manipulative troublemaker. Known for his outrageous behavior and offensive humor, Cartman frequently gets himself and his friends into absurd and hilarious situations.

X

Kenny McCormick

Kenny McCormick is known for his iconic orange parka and frequent, absurd deaths, which have become a running joke on South Park. Despite his rough upbringing, Kenny is a loyal friend and often the most daring of the group. He also adopts the persona of Mysterion, a masked vigilante who fights crime and seeks justice in South Park. As Mysterion, Kenny's character takes on a more serious tone, showcasing a hidden depth behind his usual comedic role.

X

Butters Stotch

Butters Stotch is the group's sweet and naive friend. Often the target of Cartman’s schemes, Butters' innocence and gullibility make him a lovable and comedic character in South Park.

X

Randy Marsh

Randy Marsh is the father of Stan Marsh and one of the most eccentric and hilarious adult characters in South Park. A geologist by profession, Randy often gets involved in outlandish schemes and absurd adventures, usually with a complete lack of common sense. His antics, ranging from starting businesses to leading crusades, provide some of the show’s funniest moments.
